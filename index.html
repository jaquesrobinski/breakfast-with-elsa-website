Breakfast With Elsa

– A Guide to Eggs & Bacon –

By Jack Robertson

Chef Elsa

Welcome to "Breakfast with Elsa" where you can learn how to cook bacon & eggs just like breakfast extraordinaire, Elsa Robertson. Elsa's passion for eggs & bacon developed at an early age – it was the first meal her parents taught her how to cook when she was younger. Since then, Elsa has mastered the art of eggs & bacon, cooking them in a variety of different styles. Breakfast is without a doubt Elsa's favourite meal of the day; she says that she enjoys the ritual of cooking, much like the ritual of making a coffee.

What styles of eggs & bacon can Elsa cook?

Elsa can cook eggs poached, scrambled and fried. Her talents also extend to other breakfast treats including french toast. In addition to eggs and bacon, Elsa will often add a selection of the following sides: tomatoes, mushrooms, hashbrowns, avocado, sausage, spinach, beans, onion, and many more – all of which can be served on buttered toast.

What is Elsa's favourite style of eggs & bacon?

Elsa's go-to breakfast is a poached egg with bacon. She uses the tried and tested method of cracking an egg into cling film, wrapping it up and putting it into boiling water for 5 minutes. Watch the video below to follow along with Elsa as she cooks this for breakfast.

According to Elsa, her preferred breakfast of choice is eggs and bacon as they fill her up and fuel her for the rest of the day. They are a tasty way to start the day and can be cooked in as little as 15 minutes. If Elsa was too quick for you, follow along at your own pace with her instructions below.
